# Insurance-Analytics-Dashboard
This project involves building an interactive dashboard to analyze insurance branch performance and sales KPIs.

The dashboard is designed to support discussions between the corporate team and branch managers by providing a clear view of business performance, including new, renewal, and cross-sell policies.
# Objectives
- Monitor branch-wise business performance
- Track new, renewal, and cross-sell insurance policies
- Analyze revenue, profit, and growth metrics
- Identify high-performing and underperforming branches
- Support data-driven decision-making

# Tools & Technologies
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Modeling
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Tableau
# Key Insights
- 🔁 Renewal Policies
- Exceptional performance with high profitability and growth
Strong contribution to overall business
High profit margin and growth rate
- 🆕 New Policies
- Steady growth with moderate profit margin
Requires improvement in growth rate
- 🔄 Cross-Sell Policies
- High profitability and good growth
Strong opportunity area for business expansion
# Conclusion
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of insurance business performance across branches. It highlights key areas such as renewals, new business, and cross-selling, enabling better strategic decisions.
